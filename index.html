FAQ

What size fenders do I need?

Start with boat length + freeboard, then adjust for dock type (pilings vs floating) and typical wind/current. When in doubt, a larger diameter generally offers more protection—if you can still stow them.

Are covers worth it?

Covers reduce scuffs and squeaks, and can extend the cosmetic life of your fenders.

Why do welded seams matter?

Several premium brands emphasize welded construction (vs glued) for long-term durability and air retention.
